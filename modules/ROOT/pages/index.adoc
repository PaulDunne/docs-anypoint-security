= Anypoint Security
ifndef::env-site,env-github[]
include::_attributes.adoc[]
endif::[]

Anypoint Security is a suite of features designed to protect your deployed applications using TLS/SNI validation for your endpoints, management and secure storage of your secrets such as username/password or keystores, and policies to detect and prevent external threads.

Anypoint Security uses Edge, a high-performance, reliable, and scalable service to enforce policies for DDoS (distributed denial of service), CAP (content attack prevention), and TLS/SNI Termination.
